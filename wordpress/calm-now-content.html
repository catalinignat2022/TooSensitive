🫁 Emergency Breathing

4-7-8 Technique (Do it NOW!)

  1. Breathe IN through nose for 4 counts
  2. HOLD your breath for 7 counts
  3. Breathe OUT through mouth for 8 counts
  4. REPEAT 4 times minimum

⏰ Works in 2 minutes!
